## Ingredients

- 3 avocados, mashed
- 1 lime, juiced
- 1 tsp salt
- 1/2 cup diced onion
- 3 tbsp chopped cilantro
- 2 roma tomatoes, diced
- 1 tsp minced garlic
- 1 pinch cayenne pepper (optional)

## Steps

1. Mash and mix everything together. Serve immediately.

## Notes

Holds in fridge for about 3 days before it starts turning weird colors.
